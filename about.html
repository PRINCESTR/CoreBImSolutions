OUR STORY

Built by AEC Professionals,
For AEC Professionals

Founded in 2014, Core BIM Solutions was born from a simple insight: the best architecture firms in the world shouldn't be limited by their production capacity. We exist to change that.

OUR BEGINNING

From a Single Studio to a Global Production Powerhouse

Core BIM Solutions was founded by two architects who had lived the problem firsthand — brilliant design visions constrained by the sheer volume of documentation required to bring them to life. They set out to build a different kind of technical partner.

Starting with a team of five Revit specialists in 2014, we grew methodically, client by client, always prioritizing quality over scale. Today, our 40+ person team supports architecture, engineering, and construction firms across 12 countries — and counting.

What hasn't changed is our founding principle: every drawing that leaves our team should make your project better, not just faster.

OUR PURPOSE

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every decision we make, every drawing we deliver.

Our Mission

"To eliminate production bottlenecks for the world's best architecture and construction firms — empowering their teams to focus on creativity, innovation, and client relationships while we handle the precision work that makes great design real."

Our Vision

"To become the world's most trusted on-demand BIM production partner — a seamless extension of every great firm's team, contributing to a built environment that is better designed, better coordinated, and better built."

WHAT DRIVES US

Our Core Values

Six principles embedded in every file, every deadline, every relationship.

Precision Above All

Every line, every dimension, every coordinate matters. We treat accuracy as a non-negotiable — not a goal, but a baseline.

True Partnership

We don't see ourselves as vendors. We're an extension of your team — invested in your project's success as deeply as you are.

Speed Without Compromise

Our global team structure allows us to deliver fast — often overnight — without ever trading quality for speed.

Continuous Innovation

BIM technology evolves rapidly. We invest in constant training, software, and process innovation to keep our clients ahead.

Confidentiality

We understand the sensitivity of design work. Every engagement begins with an NDA, and data security is built into our entire workflow.

Global Mindset

Our diverse, internationally distributed team brings cross-cultural expertise to every project — understanding local building codes, standards, and client expectations worldwide.
