HOW WE WORK

Seamless Integration.
Zero Disruption.

Our 8-step engagement process integrates with your existing workflow from day one — no learning curve, no onboarding friction.

THE PROCESS

8 Steps to
Production Excellence

01

Project Brief

Share scope, timeline, standards, and any reference files. We confirm feasibility and assign your dedicated PM.

02

NDA & Agreement

We sign a mutual NDA before any files are shared. Simple, no lock-in contracts for project duration only.

03

Standards Alignment

Your PM reviews templates, layer standards, naming conventions. We set up a matching workspace environment.

04

Fixed-Price Quote

Quote provided within 24 hours — scope, deliverables, timeline, revision rounds. No hourly billing surprises.

05

Production

Our certified specialists execute production — overnight if needed. Parallel work packages maximize speed.

06

Internal QA

Multi-stage quality review before you see anything. Accuracy, standard compliance, file health all verified.

07

Client Review

Files delivered with structured feedback system. Annotate directly on drawings using your preferred platform.

08

Final Delivery

Approved files in your format — DWG, RVT, PDF, IFC. Project archived for fast reactivation on future phases.
