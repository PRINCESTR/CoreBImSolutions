Downtown Tower:
LOD 400 BIM in 8 Weeks

A 42-story mixed-use tower. One overloaded internal team. Eight weeks to permit. Here is how we stepped in and delivered.

The Challenge

Sterling & Co. won the commission for a landmark 42-story mixed-use tower in downtown Chicago. Their BIM team was already at capacity on two other major projects, with no room to absorb a 480,000 sqft tower requiring LOD 400 delivery in 8 weeks.

Our Approach

We deployed 8 certified BIM specialists, divided the model into 14 parallel work packages, and leveraged time zones to deliver overnight progress. Daily PM syncs with Sterling kept quality and scope aligned throughout. Navisworks coordination sessions every Friday resolved the week's clashes.

"Core BIM delivered in 8 weeks what would have taken our team 16. The model quality was indistinguishable from our internal work — and in some areas, better."

— Marcus Sterling, Principal Architect

The Results

LOD 400 model delivered 3 days ahead of deadline. Permit approved on first submission. Zero RFI-worthy clashes during construction. Sterling saved $280K vs. the cost of dedicated contract staff.
